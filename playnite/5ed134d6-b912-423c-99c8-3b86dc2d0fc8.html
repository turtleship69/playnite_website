STORY

Zoria: Age of Shattering Prologue is an introduction into the world of Zoria. Follow the events that lead to Captain Andar taking charge of the Outpost, the recruiting of its first followers and the stories and events that happen along the way. These stories, together, paint a picture of the world and prepare you for the events that will unfold once Zoria: Age of Shattering is released.


GAMEPLAY

Zoria’s gameplay is a unique mix of action-adventure RPG and tactical management. Explore the beautiful lands of Zoria, survive epic battles, delve into dangerous dungeons, and follow the story unfolding.

Zoria: Age of Shattering is a story-driven, party-based Classic RPG with base and character management. The player has of a party of followers, each with their special skills and attributes, adding a tactical layer to Every action taken.

Explore the beautiful lands of Zoria, from forests to caves and dungeons, witness the story unfold and discover all its secrets.


ADVENTURE

All the places in the game are hand-crafted, with multiple paths and various options for exploration, depending on the party setup and their exploration abilities


RESOURCE MANAGEMENT

Exploring the world needs to be planned out carefully, and supplies stocked accordingly. Without supplies, the party cannot restore their vital resources, making any dungeon that much more dangerous. Acquiring enough supplies along the way is vital for the success of the party.


TURN-BASED COMBAT

Zoria’s combat is a Tactical, Turn-based confrontation, designed around the attributes of each class: special abilities, resistances, deadly ranged attacks or strong AOE defenses, healing and much more. But Zoria is a living world, so choose your battlegrounds wisely, as any wandering creature or enemy that gets too close might join in and ruin your best laid plans.


CHARACTERS

Build up your party by recruiting followers as you progress along in your adventure. Each follower and each class have a unique skill set, both in combat and exploring the world. Magic, ranged attacks, sheer power, blocks and stuns, bleed and poisons, magic resistances - all there to explore.

  • An epic story driving your experience, with rich and expansive lore. Discover the hidden tales and stories that live on through the people of Zoria and your own companions;
  • A unique experience following the events leading to the story of Zoria: Age of Shattering.
